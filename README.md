# Sales Performance and Customer Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance and customer behavior. The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, product categories, brands, cities, payment methods, and order status to support business decision-making.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel / CSV Dataset
- Data Modeling
- DAX Measures

## Dataset
The dataset contains over 1500 transaction records from an Indian retail business including information about:

- Brand
- Category
- City
- Customer
- Payment Method
- Order Status
- Quantity
- Unit Price
- Discount
- Tax
- Shipping Cost
- Total Amount

## Key KPIs
- Total Sales
- Total Quantity
- Average Unit Price
- Total Discount
- Total Shipping Cost
- Total Tax

## Dashboard Features
- Interactive slicers for City, Category, State, Payment Method, and Order Status
- Sales distribution by category and city
- Brand performance analysis
- Quantity analysis by brand and category
- Average unit price comparison across cities

## Key Insights
- Mumbai shows the highest sales and average unit price.
- Electronics and Laptops contribute more than 50% of total sales.
- HP and Apple lead in sales quantity among brands.
- Discounts and shipping costs impact total revenue.

## Future Improvements
